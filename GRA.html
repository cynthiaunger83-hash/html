The Graphic Media Design program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world creative careers by teaching how to design digital and printed media. Students learn to create things like logos, posters, websites, and advertisements that businesses use. In this program, students get hands-on experience using design software and tools used by professionals. They also learn creativity, communication, and how to meet deadlines, which are important skills in the design industry. Overall, the program helps students build practical skills they can use after graduation. They can work in graphic design, marketing, or media, or continue their education in art and design fields.
