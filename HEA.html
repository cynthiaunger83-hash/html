what is HEA?

It focuses on teaching you how to care for patients and assist in medical settings like hospitals, clinics, and nursing homes.

The Health Assistant program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world careers in healthcare. Students learn skills like taking vital signs, assisting patients, basic medical procedures, and understanding how healthcare settings work. In this program, students often get hands-on experience in labs or clinical settings. They also learn communication, teamwork, and professionalism, which are very important when working with patients and medical staff. Overall, the program helps students build practical skills they can use after graduation. They can go into entry-level healthcare jobs, continue their education in nursing or other medical fields, or pursue certifications in healthcare.
