what is COS?

is a career training program that teaches the students how to work in the beauty industry

The Cosmetology program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world careers in beauty and personal care. Students learn skills like cutting and styling hair, coloring, skincare, and nail care, which are all used in salons. In this program, students get hands-on experience working with real tools and sometimes real clients in a salon-like setting. They also learn hygiene, safety, customer service, and communication, which are important for working with people. Overall, the program helps students build practical skills they can use after graduation. They can work toward getting a cosmetology license, start a job in a salon, or continue their education in the beauty industry.
