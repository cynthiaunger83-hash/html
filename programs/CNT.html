what is CNT?

building on foundations and farming. the construction trades program is a comprehensive three year track that turns highschoolers into skilled builders.

The Construction Trades program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world work by teaching the skills needed to build and repair structures. Students learn things like framing, carpentry, measuring, and using tools, which are all important in construction jobs. In this program, students get hands-on experience working with materials like wood and concrete while following safety rules and reading basic plans. They also learn teamwork, problem-solving, and responsibility, which are important on any job site. Overall, the program helps students gain practical skills they can use right after graduation. They can go straight into construction jobs, start an apprenticeship, or continue learning in a trade school or college.
