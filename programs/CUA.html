what is CUA?

it is a hands-on training program that twaches students how to cook professionally and work in the food industry.

The Culinary, Baking, and Pastry Arts program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world careers in the food industry. Students learn how to cook meals, bake breads and desserts, and create pastries, while also understanding kitchen safety and food handling. In this program, students get hands-on experience working in a kitchen setting using professional equipment. They learn skills like measuring ingredients, following recipes, time management, and teamwork, which are all important in restaurants and bakeries. Overall, the program helps students build practical skills they can use after graduation. They can work in restaurants, bakeries, or hotels, or continue their education in culinary arts.
