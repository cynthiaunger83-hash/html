what is STS?

The Sports Therapy & Exercise Science program focuses on how the body moves, how to improve fitness, and how to prevent and treat injuries related to sports and exercise.

The Sports Therapy and Exercise Science program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world careers focused on fitness, health, and sports performance. Students learn how the body moves, how to prevent injuries, and how to help people improve strength, flexibility, and overall fitness. In this program, students get hands-on experience with exercise routines, fitness testing, and basic athletic training techniques. They also learn communication, teamwork, and how to support people safely during physical activity, which are important in sports and healthcare settings. Overall, the program helps students build practical skills they can use after graduation. They can go into fitness training, athletic coaching, physical therapy support roles, or continue their education in sports science, health, or exercise-related fields.
