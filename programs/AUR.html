what is AUR?

it teaches students how to restore, repair, amd refinish damaged vehicals. unlike genral atuomotive technology, which focuses on mechanics, AUR speclizes in the "art" of vehical aesthetuics and stuctual integrety.

The Automotive Body Repair program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world work by teaching how to fix and restore damaged vehicles. Students learn skills like repairing dents, replacing parts, sanding, painting, and refinishing cars so they look like new again. In this program, students get hands-on experience using real tools and equipment in a shop setting. They also learn safety, attention to detail, and how to follow repair procedures, which are important for working in an auto body shop. Overall, the program helps students gain practical skills and experience they can use right after graduation. They can go straight into a job, continue training, or work toward certifications in the automotive repair field.
