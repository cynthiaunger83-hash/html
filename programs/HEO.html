what is HEO?

It focuses on teaching you how to safely use and repair heavy machinery like bulldozers, excavators, and loaders.

The Heavy Equipment Operation and Repair program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world careers working with large machines used in construction and industry. Students learn how to operate equipment like loaders, bulldozers, and excavators, as well as how to maintain and repair them. In this program, students get hands-on experience using real equipment and tools. They also learn safety, problem-solving, and how to inspect and fix mechanical issues, which are important skills for the job. Overall, the program helps students build practical skills they can use after graduation. They can go straight into jobs, start an apprenticeship, or continue their education in heavy equipment or diesel technology fields.
