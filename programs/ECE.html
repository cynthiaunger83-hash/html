what is ECE?

it foucuses on teaching you how to support and educate young children.

The Early Childhood Education program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world careers working with young children. Students learn how children grow and develop, how to plan activities, and how to create a safe and positive learning environment. In this program, students often get hands-on experience by working with children in classroom or daycare settings. They also learn communication, patience, and responsibility, which are very important when working with kids. Overall, the program helps students build practical skills they can use after graduation. They can work in childcare centers, continue their education to become a teacher, or pursue other careers related to education and child development.
