what is MFT?

The Metal Fabrication Technology program teaches you how to cut, shape, and build things out of metal using tools, machines, and welding techniques.

The Metal Fabrication Technology program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world careers by teaching how to cut, shape, and join metal to create or repair products. Students learn skills like welding, measuring, reading blueprints, and using industrial tools. In this program, students get hands-on experience working with different metals and equipment used in fabrication shops. They also learn safety, precision, and problem-solving, which are very important in manufacturing and construction jobs. Overall, the program helps students build practical skills they can use after graduation. They can go straight into welding or fabrication jobs, start an apprenticeship, or continue their education in skilled trades or engineering fields.
