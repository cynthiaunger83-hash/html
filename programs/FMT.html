what is FMT?

it trains you to maintain, repair, and manage buildings and their systems so everything runs safley and efficiently.

The Facility Maintenance Technology program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world jobs by teaching how to maintain and repair buildings. Students learn skills like basic plumbing, electrical work, heating and cooling systems, and general repairs. In this program, students get hands-on experience using tools and equipment to fix and maintain different parts of a building. They also learn problem-solving, safety, and responsibility, which are important for keeping facilities running smoothly. Overall, the program helps students build practical skills they can use after graduation. They can go straight into maintenance jobs, start an apprenticeship, or continue their education in a trade or technical field.
