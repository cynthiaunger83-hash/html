what is CMP?

it focuses on the development of software soulutions for business and technical challanges. it is a rigorous course of study that transitions from basic programming logic to advance application development across multiple platforms.

The Computer Programming program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world tech careers by teaching them how to write and understand code. Students learn programming languages and how to create software, apps, and websites, which are important skills in today’s digital world. In this program, students get hands-on experience by building their own projects and solving problems using code. They also learn logical thinking, debugging, and how to work on projects step by step—skills that are important for any programming job. Overall, the program helps students gain practical skills they can use after graduation. They can go straight into an entry-level tech job, continue their education, or pursue careers in software development and other technology fields.
