what is ADS?

it is a course that bridges traditional fine arts with modern digital media and professional business practices it is designed to prepare high school students for careers in commercial arts or further education in multimedia and design

The Art and Design for Business course at Erie County Technical School helps students prepare for real-world careers by teaching both creative skills and how they are used in business. Students learn how to design things like logos, advertisements, and digital graphics that companies actually need, so their work has real purpose. In this course, students use professional tools and software to create designs, while also learning important skills like communication, meeting deadlines, and working with clients. This helps them understand what it’s like to have a job in graphic design or marketing. Overall, the course prepares students for the future by giving them hands-on experience and practical skills they can use right after graduation—whether they go straight into a job, freelance work, or continue studying design in college.
