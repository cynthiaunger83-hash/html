what is AMT?

focuses on the precision design and production of metal and plactic components. it combines hands-on mechanical training with computer-aided technology to prepare students for carees in modren industires.

The Advanced Manufacturing Technology program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world jobs by teaching how modern products are made using machines and technology. Students learn to operate equipment, read blueprints, and understand how manufacturing systems work, which are all important skills in factories and production settings. In this program, students get hands-on experience with tools, machines, and sometimes computer-controlled equipment. They also learn problem-solving, teamwork, and safety skills, which are important in any workplace. This helps them feel ready to work in a real manufacturing environment. Overall, the program helps students build practical skills they can use right after graduation. They can go straight into a job, start an apprenticeship, or continue their education in manufacturing or engineering.
