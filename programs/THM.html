what is THM?

The Hospitality Management & Tourism program teaches you how to manage businesses that serve guests and travelers, like hotels, restaurants, and tourism companies.

The Hospitality Management and Tourism program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world careers in hotels, restaurants, travel, and customer service. Students learn how the hospitality industry works, including how to help guests, manage events, and support tourism services. In this program, students get hands-on experience with skills like customer service, communication, teamwork, and planning events. They also learn how to handle real-world situations in places like hotels and restaurants, which helps them understand professional expectations. Overall, the program helps students build practical skills they can use after graduation. They can work in hotels, restaurants, travel agencies, or continue their education in hospitality, business, or tourism fields.
