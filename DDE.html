what is DDE?

teaches students how to design and create technical drawings used to build thing likes houses, machines, and products.

The Drafting and Design Engineering program at Erie County Technical School prepares students for real-world careers by teaching how to create technical drawings and designs. Students learn how to design buildings, machines, and parts using computer software and precise measurements. In this program, students get hands-on experience with design tools and programs used by professionals, like CAD (computer-aided design). They also learn problem-solving, attention to detail, and how to read and create blueprints, which are important skills in engineering and architecture fields. Overall, the program helps students build practical skills they can use after graduation. They can go into drafting or design jobs, continue their education in engineering, or work in fields like architecture or manufacturing.
